Jana is an architect and urban researcher engaging in topics of national urban planning practices, housing policies and displacement, urban transformation, and history. Jana completed her MA in Architecture at the Faculty of Architecture and Fine Arts of the Lebanese University. Her area of study and general interest has influenced Jana’s professional track from an inquisitive city dweller, to a lead urban researcher turned project manager, working with local research institutions. She is also the creator of Beirut Architecture Tours (2019), and later MASARAT (2024), a walking tour project that explores stories of placemaking in Beirut.
